Taco Trivia

Where did Taco word come from?

Did you know that the word "taco" comes from the Mexican Spanish word "taco," which means "plug" or "wad"? The word was used to describe the pieces of paper that were wrapped around gunpowder and inserted into holes in the rock to blast out ore in silver mines. The word was later used to describe the pieces of paper that were wrapped around food to hold it together, and eventually came to refer to the food itself.
